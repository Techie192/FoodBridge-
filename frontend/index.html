yard Our Mission

Bridging the gap between abundance and need

Every day, tons of perfectly good food are thrown away while millions go hungry. FoodBridge was created to solve this logistical challenge. We provide a seamless, real-time platform connecting restaurants, bakeries, and households with local NGOs and shelters. By making food rescue effortless, we are building a world with zero hunger and zero waste.

✦ The Process

How it works

A simple, three-step framework to mobilize surplus food — fast, transparent, and impactful.

01

List surplus

Restaurants, caterers, or households post available food with quantity, type, and pickup window.

02

Match & claim

Nearby NGOs, shelters, and verified recipients browse real-time listings and claim what they need.

03

Pickup & confirm

Donor and recipient coordinate pickup. Both confirm completion to track real, measurable impact.
